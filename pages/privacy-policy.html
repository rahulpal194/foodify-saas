Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Foodify collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use our platform, website, and related services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, business name, phone number, and other registration details.
  • Business Data: Menu items, orders, transactions, and sales reports
  • Payment Information: Billing address and payment method (we do not store credit card data; it’s processed by secure third-party providers).
  • Usage Data: Device information, IP address, browser type, and activity logs

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Foodify platform.
  • Personalize your experience and improve our services.
  • Communicate with you regarding support, updates, or marketing (with consent).
  • Monitor usage to detect and prevent fraud or misuse.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist in hosting, payment processing, or analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and remember preferences. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access or update your data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to certain data uses.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.

Contact privacy@foodifyapp.com to exercise any of these rights.

Third-Party Links

Foodify may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

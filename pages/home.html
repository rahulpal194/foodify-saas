Tired of Juggling Multiple Systems?

Foodify replaces your patchwork of apps with one streamlined SaaS platform that does it all—whether you run a single café or a multi-branch restaurant chain.

Smart, Reliable POS & Billing for Modern Restaurants

Handle orders and payments with speed and accuracy using Foodify’s cloud-based POS system. Whether it’s dine-in, takeaway, or delivery, our intuitive interface ensures fast billing, real-time syncing, and error-free transactions—accessible from any device, anytime.

Zone-Based Delivery & Takeaway —All in One Platform

Simplify your operations with Foodify’s built-in Delivery & Takeaway system. Manage zone-wise delivery assignments, track orders in real-time. Whether customers choose doorstep delivery or a quick pickup, Foodify ensures a smooth, efficient experience from kitchen to customer.

Smart QR Menu Ordering for a Seamless Dining Experience

Let your customers browse the menu, place orders, and pay—right from their phones. With Foodify’s QR code ordering system, there’s no need for printed menus or apps. It’s fast, and fully integrated with your kitchen and POS, giving guests a self-service experience while reducing staff workload.

Centralized Menu & Order Management from Anywhere

Easily create, customize, and manage your entire menu from one dashboard. With Foodify, you can update prices, add item variations, and organize categories in real time—across all branches. Orders flow seamlessly from customers to the kitchen, ensuring faster service for both staff and customers.

From Counter to Kitchen — Orders in Sync with KDS

Eliminate paper tickets and speed up kitchen efficiency with Foodify’s real-time Kitchen Display System. Orders are instantly synced from POS or customer apps to the kitchen screen. With status tracking, and auto-update features, your kitchen staff stays organized, focused, and fast.
